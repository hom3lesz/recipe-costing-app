Instant GP Calculation

AI Method Writer

Generate professional step-by-step cooking methods with one click. Supports Claude, Gemini Flash, and local Ollama models for complete privacy.

Multi-Site Management

Manage multiple kitchen locations from one app. Each site has its own recipe library, supplier contacts and GP targets.

Print polished recipe cards with per-portion nutrition, allergens, cost and GP — perfect for kitchen folders and staff training.
